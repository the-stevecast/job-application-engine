# Target Roles

> **How the agent uses this file:** Classifies incoming job descriptions into your defined lanes, then adjusts competency ordering, bullet selection, and cover letter angle based on the lane. The better you define your lanes, the more precisely the agent can tailor each application.
>
> **How to populate:** Run `/setup` or `/setup targeting`. Or fill in directly.

---

## Example (for reference)

> ### Lane Product Management — Core PM roles at growth-stage companies
>
> **Titles:** Senior Product Manager, Group Product Manager, Product Lead, Director of Product
>
> **Core value I bring:** I turn ambiguous problems into shipped products with measurable outcomes. I can lead discovery, write specs, and drive cross-functional alignment without needing heavy process.
>
> **Positioning emphasis:** Outcome track record, discovery methodology, cross-functional leadership, comfort with ambiguity
>
> ---
>
> ### Lane Platform / Technical PM — Roles requiring deep technical depth or infrastructure focus
>
> **Titles:** Platform PM, Technical Product Manager, API PM, Infrastructure PM
>
> **Core value I bring:** I can work directly with engineering on platform decisions and translate technical trade-offs into business framing for leadership.
>
> **Positioning emphasis:** Technical credibility, API/systems thinking, engineering partnership, developer experience
>
